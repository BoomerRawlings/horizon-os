# Index

Welcome to your Horizon vault. Start here.

- **Calendar/** — your dated items and the rolling `Now.md`.
- **Inbox/** — captures land here first, then you triage them in the app.
- **Project Registry/** — one note per project.
- **Research Papers/** — local paper notes and reading material.
- **Runs/** — logs the app writes automatically.

New to Horizon? Open the app and follow the **Getting Started** walkthrough, or read
**Settings → Advanced → How to use Horizon** any time.
